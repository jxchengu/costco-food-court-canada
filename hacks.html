Root Beer Float/ Coke float

If you're local Costco Food court has Barq's root beer, get a vanilla soft serve and combine them for a classic root beer float.

For a Coke float, simply substitute the root beer with Coca-Cola whch will give you a soda float.

Lastly, If your location has Fanta, combine it with the vanilla soft serve to make an "orange dreamsicle" style float.

Root Beer Float / Coca Cola float / Orange Creamsicle
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Any type of Coca Cola/ root beer / Fanta
  • Put the ice cream in the soda

The Costco Taco

This fun combination requires either the polish or beef costco hot dog and a slice of pizza of your choice. Wrap the hot dog in the pizza to make a "taco".

The Costco Taco
  • Costco Hot dog (Polish or Beef)
  • Any type of Pizza
The Costco Taco
  • Remove the beef sausage from the bread
  • Add any type of condiments such as ketchup or mustard
  • Place the hot dog in the pizza
  • Roll the pizza around the sausage

The Poutine Hack

If you're trying to save money, order the Costco Chicken Strips and Fries, a hot cup of gravy and cheese curds. Get a poutine container from the staff, put the cheese curds on the fries and then pour the rich and hot gravy on top to create a poutine.

Poutine Hack
  • Costco Chicken strips & Fries
  • Hot cup of gravy
  • Cheese curds
  • Condiments if needed
How to make it
  • Get a poutine container from the staff
  • Put the cheese curds on the fries & chicken
  • Pour the hot gravy on top

The Ice cream sandwich

If you're feeling hot, go to the Costco food court to order any ice cream cup of your choice and two double chunk chocolate cookies. Put the ice cream on the two cookies to create an "ice cream sandwich".

Ice Cream Sandwich
  • Two Double Chocolate Chunk Cookies
  • Any ice cream of your choice
Instructions
  • Take one cookie and place a scoop of ice cream on top.
  • Top with the second cookie and press down gently.
